TRAINING AND PLACEMENT CELL

Our Vision

The vision of the Placement Cell is to serve as a vital bridge between academic excellence and industry expectations, equipping students with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities needed to thrive in their chosen careers. It aspires to build strong and lasting professional partnerships that enhance the institution's reputation and contribute to the holistic development of its students.

Our Mission

The mission of the Placement Cell is to prepare students for the competitive job market by enhancing their skills, fostering industry collaborations for placements and internships, and nurturing innovation, adaptability, and lifelong learning for holistic career success.
