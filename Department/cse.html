Vision

We at UCET are dedicated to providing state-of-the-art technical education driven by sustainability, innovation, and moral responsibility. By incorporating cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, the Internet of Things, and sustainable engineering into our curriculum, we hope to establish international standards with our committed faculty. Our goal is to develop technologically savvy, socially conscious, and morally motivated people who will lead the way in developing game-changing solutions that will improve humankind's quality of life and create a more intelligent, sustainable future.

Mission

About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in University College of Engineering in Tindivanam in the year 2008 with the objective of imparting quality education in the field of Computer Science. With rapidly evolving technology and the continuous need for innovation, the department has always produced quality professionals holding important positions in software industry and banking sector in India and Abroad. The department has started B.E. Computer Science and Engineering course in the year 2008 with the aim to develop core competence in Computer Science and Engineering and prepare the students to carry out programming development work as well as to take up the challenges of research work. Moreover, all the computers are fully connected in the LAN network that provides 8 Mbps leased line Internet connection.

Message from the HOD

Dr.L.JEGATHA DEBORAH

HOD (i/c), AP/CSE

Dr. L. Jegatha Deborah is the Head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. Until the academic year 2009-2010, she managed both the Computer Science and Engineering and Information Technology departments. Since then, she has focused solely on Computer Science and Engineering. With over eight years of teaching experience, her research spans Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Databases. Dr. Deborah graduated with a degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Madurai Kamaraj University in 2002, completed her postgraduate studies at Anna University in 2005, and earned her Ph.D. in Information and Communication Engineering from Anna University in March 2013. Her academic interests include Database Systems, Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, and Natural Language Processing. She has published numerous research papers in reputed national and international conferences and journals and has attended several international conferences.
